What I do

From chaos
to clarity.

Most small business owners hit a wall where hard work and good intentions aren't enough anymore. The business needs real systems to grow, and building those systems is exactly what I do.

I work primarily with owners of lean, 1–5 person teams who are ready to get organized, delegate with confidence, and stop being the only person who knows how things work.

01

Operations mapping

We look at how your business actually runs today (not how you think it does) and identify every place where things break down or depend on you personally.

02

Role clarity & team structure

Who owns what? When tasks live with roles rather than people, delegation stops being scary and starts working.

03

Systems & automation

From workflow documentation to software integrations, I build the infrastructure that lets your team run without you having to orchestrate every piece.

04

Ongoing support

Some engagements are a sprint; others are a longer partnership. Either way, I'm in the weeds with you, not handing you a deck and walking away.
